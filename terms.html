2. What Joles is

Joles is a WhatsApp-based assistant that helps you manage a family calendar: adding, editing, and tracking events and tasks by text, voice note, or photo/document, and sending digests and reminders to your household.

Joles uses AI to interpret your messages. Like any AI system, it can occasionally misread, mis-transcribe, or misinterpret what you send — for example, extracting the wrong date from a photo, or mishearing a voice note. Please always confirm important events (appointments, deadlines, school events) by checking your calendar directly, especially for anything time-sensitive. We are not liable for missed appointments or events resulting from AI misinterpretation; you remain responsible for verifying critical scheduling information.
