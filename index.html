Joles, your WhatsApp-based family scheduler, is here to help! Text, send a voice note, or simply snap a screenshot of that Parents Gateway notice and let Joles do the scheduling for you — in English or 中文!

No app to install. No calendar to maintain.

Joles is a WhatsApp contact your whole family messages — the same way you already message each other.

1

Message it like you'd message anyone

Forward the Parents Gateway notice, screenshot the ClassDojo update, snap the tuition timetable, or just type "Ashton's swim class moved to 4pm Sat."

2

It keeps everything straight

Joles reads it, pulls out the dates and details, and asks you to confirm before anything goes on the calendar. Nothing gets added behind your back.

3

Everyone stays in sync

When one parent adds or changes something, the other hears about it. Every evening, everyone gets tomorrow's schedule — before it's tomorrow.

Snap the notice. Skip the typing.

It shows you what it found, you reply once, and it's all on the calendar.
